gen_spectra
==================

.. py:module::src.gen_spectra

This module does anything and everything with mass and spectra generation. The 
actual masses of amino acids, singly and doubly charged b and y ions as well as 
the mass of a proton and water are kept in the constants file in the src 
directory.
